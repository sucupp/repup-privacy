Common questions

Rep counting isn't working. Make sure the camera can see your whole body, you're in a well-lit room, and RepUp has camera permission (Settings → RepUp → Camera). All processing is on-device — nothing is recorded.

How do I restore my subscription? Open RepUp → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID you purchased with.

How do I cancel? Manage or cancel anytime in your iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

Free trial. RepUp Premium includes a 3-day free trial on the yearly plan. You won't be charged if you cancel before it ends.
